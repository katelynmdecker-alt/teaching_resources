Lesson 5: Translations of Trigonometric Graphs

Interact with the fan to see how the sine wave changes in real-time

Purpose: In this lesson, students will explore how vertical shifts (midline), changes in amplitude, and phase shifts simultaneously affect the graph of a periodic function. By interacting with the fan and its resulting graph, students can visualize and understand the combined effects of these mathematical transformations.
